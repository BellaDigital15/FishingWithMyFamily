Fishing with my family

Here is where the salmon has not only sustained my family for many generations, but for generations to come because we had lived and abided by the principles of ethical harvesting. We only kept our catch from fishing by never harvesting more fish than our family can consume. Much like a fishing crew, our family works together to preserve our salmon in varioius ways of drying, smoking, barbequing, jarring, canning, vaccuum packing.We harvest, preserve and eat meals together. My name is Georgia Larsen, I started my family with my husband Ian Larsen and together our son completes our family - Cassian.
